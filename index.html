KQJ Match

Hypercasual/casual match 3 card game

KQJ Match was developed recently for Voodoo publishing pitches.. with over 25% D1 Retention and average of 8m20sec of gameplay. KQJ Matach has simple yet captivating game mechanics, its a match 3 card game. Player are given goals that is random each level and increasing in difficulty as level progress.

Level generation are Inifinite. A New level button will be added each time player completes the previous level.

DotWeen plugin was really helpful to make the UI and player experience more interesting.
